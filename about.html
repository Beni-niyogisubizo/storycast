Accessibility Commitment

Accessibility isn't an afterthought at StoryCast it's foundational. Here's how we ensure our content and platform are usable by everyone.

  • Transcripts for All Audio

    Every audio story includes a full, time-stamped transcript so deaf and hard-of-hearing audiences can engage with the content.

  • Captions for All Video

    All video content includes synchronized captions. We're working toward descriptive audio tracks for blind and low-vision users.

  • Keyboard Navigation

    The entire site is fully navigable via keyboard. All interactive elements have visible focus indicators that meet WCAG 2.1 AA contrast requirements.

  • Semantic & Structured

    We use semantic HTML5 and ARIA attributes so screen readers and assistive technologies can accurately convey the structure and meaning of our content.
